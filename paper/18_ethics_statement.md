# 18. Ethics Statement

Decision Security Engineering is intended to improve the safety, trustworthiness, accountability, and governance of autonomous AI systems.

The framework is designed to support responsible deployment of AI by introducing runtime validation mechanisms prior to autonomous execution.

The authors recognize that security technologies may be applied in ways that influence operational decisions affecting individuals and organizations. Implementations should therefore incorporate appropriate human oversight, transparency, accountability, privacy protections, and compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

This work is presented as an engineering framework intended to encourage safer autonomous systems rather than increase autonomous authority without oversight.

Future implementations should be evaluated for fairness, reliability, explainability, privacy, and societal impact.
