Behavioral Identity For Autonomous Agents

Agent DNA™ models stable behavioral characteristics instead of relying solely on prompts or model outputs.

Each execution is evaluated against learned behavioral invariants, policy constraints and decision history.

Unexpected mutations increase risk scores before execution is permitted.
