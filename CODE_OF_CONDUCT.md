# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The Decision Security Engineering project is committed to fostering an open, professional, and respectful research community.

We welcome participation from researchers, engineers, students, practitioners, and organizations regardless of background or affiliation.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus discussions on technical merit.
- Support claims with evidence where possible.
- Welcome thoughtful disagreement.
- Help improve the quality of the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples include:

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Discrimination of any kind.
- Deliberate misinformation.
- Spam or abusive communication.
- Disruptive or hostile behavior.

## Research Discussions

Scientific disagreement is encouraged.

Technical critiques should focus on ideas, evidence, methodology, and engineering decisions rather than individuals.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation that violates this Code of Conduct.
